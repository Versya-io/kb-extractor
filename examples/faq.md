# Frequently Asked Questions — Tabloide.pro

---

## Getting Started

### How do I create my first flyer?
Go to **My Flyers** and click **New Flyer**. Choose a template from the library,
then click **Edit** to open the editor. When you're done, click **Save** in the
top right corner.

### Do I need to create an account to use Tabloide.pro?
Yes, a free account is required. You can sign up with your email or Google account
at tabloide.pro/register. No credit card is needed to start.

### What's included in the free plan?
The free plan lets you create up to 5 flyers per month using the standard template
library. Exports include a platform watermark. To remove the watermark and access
premium templates, upgrade to a paid plan.

### How do I upload my store's logo?
Go to **Account Settings → Brand**, then click **Upload Logo**. Accepted formats
are PNG and JPG, maximum 5MB. Logo upload is available on Starter and Pro plans.
On the Free plan, the platform watermark is displayed instead.

## Common Actions

### How do I change the text on a template?
In the editor, click directly on any text element. A text editing panel will
appear on the right side. Type your new text and click outside the element to confirm.

### How do I change the background color of my flyer?
In the editor, click on an empty area of the flyer (not on any element).
The **Background** panel will appear on the right. Click the color swatch to open
the color picker.

### How do I add a product price to my flyer?
Click **Add Element → Price Tag** in the left sidebar. A price element will appear
on the canvas. Click it to edit the value, font, and color.

### How do I download my flyer?
Click **Export** in the top right of the editor. Choose your format (JPG or PNG)
and click **Download**. Free plan exports at 72dpi; paid plans export at 300dpi.

### Can I share my flyer directly to WhatsApp or Instagram?
Yes. After saving, click **Share** on the flyer card. Select the channel
(WhatsApp, Instagram, Facebook) and follow the prompts. Sharing requires the
flyer to be saved first.

### How do I duplicate a flyer I already made?
On the **My Flyers** screen, hover over the flyer card and click the **⋯** menu,
then select **Duplicate**. The copy opens in the editor ready to edit.

### How do I change the template after I've started editing?
You cannot change the base template once you've started editing — doing so would
overwrite your content. Instead, duplicate the flyer first, then start a new one
with the desired template.

## Limitations

### Can I move the logo to a different position on the flyer?
It depends on the template. In **free-layout templates**, you can drag the logo
anywhere on the canvas. In **fixed-layout templates**, the logo position is locked.
The template type is shown in the template library with a "Free" or "Fixed" badge.

### Can I remove the logo from a template that has one?
No. Templates that include a logo area require some image in that space. You can
replace it with your own logo (on paid plans) but cannot leave it empty.

### Why is the Export button grayed out?
The Export button is disabled when the flyer has unsaved changes. Click **Save**
first, then Export will become available.

### Can I use my own fonts?
Custom font upload is not currently available. The editor includes a curated
library of fonts accessible from the text panel. New fonts are added with
each platform update.

### Can I create animated flyers or GIFs?
Animated exports are not supported at this time. All exports are static JPG or PNG.

### Can I have multiple people editing the same flyer at the same time?
Real-time collaborative editing is not available. Only one person can edit a
flyer at a time. For team workflows, use the Duplicate feature to create
individual copies.

### Why can't I access some templates?
Templates marked with a lock icon (🔒) are available on Starter and Pro plans only.
Your current plan is shown in **Account → Subscription**.

### Is there a limit to how many flyers I can have saved?
Saved flyers do not count against your monthly creation limit. You can keep as
many saved flyers as you want. The monthly limit applies only to newly created flyers.

## Plans & Pricing

### What's the difference between monthly and annual plans?
Annual plans offer the same features as monthly plans at a reduced price
(billed once per year). You can switch from monthly to annual at any time
from **Account → Subscription**.

### Can I upgrade mid-month?
Yes. Upgrades take effect immediately. You'll be charged a prorated amount
for the remainder of the current billing period.

### What happens to my flyers if I downgrade to the free plan?
Your existing flyers are preserved and remain accessible. However, flyers that
used premium templates will be locked for editing until you upgrade again.
You can still download previously exported files.

### Do unused flyer credits from my monthly limit roll over?
No. The monthly creation limit resets on your billing date and does not accumulate.

## Errors & Problems

### I see "Export failed — please try again." What do I do?
This usually happens due to a temporary server issue. Wait 30 seconds and try
again. If the problem persists, try saving the flyer again before exporting.
If it still fails, contact support with the flyer name and the time of the error.

### My uploaded image looks blurry in the flyer. Why?
The source image resolution may be too low. For best results, use images at
least 1000px wide. JPG images with heavy compression also reduce quality —
use PNG when possible.

### I can't log in — I get "Invalid credentials."
Double-check that you're using the email address you registered with.
If you signed up with Google, use the **Continue with Google** button instead
of email/password. To reset your password, click **Forgot password** on the login screen.

### The editor is slow or not loading elements.
Try clearing your browser cache and reloading the page. The editor works best
on Chrome and Safari with a stable internet connection. Very large image uploads
(over 5MB) can slow down the canvas.

### I accidentally deleted a flyer. Can I recover it?
Deleted flyers are moved to **Trash** and kept for 30 days. Go to
**My Flyers → Trash** to restore them. After 30 days, deletion is permanent.

### My flyer looks different when printed vs. on screen.
Screen displays use RGB color, while printers use CMYK. Colors may shift
slightly in print, especially bright blues and greens. For best print results,
export at 300dpi (paid plans) and use a professional print service.
